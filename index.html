About

My work explores symbolism, portraiture, and heritage using the ancient technique of wood burning (pyrography). Pieces are created by hand with heat on sustainably sourced wood, often inspired by forests of northern Iran and the textures of nature.

Follow along on Instagram for works‑in‑progress and details, and on YouTube for time‑lapses and shorts.
